📚 The 4% Rule · Trinity Study

25× your annual expenses

Numbr generalizes a classic idea: if your savings earn about 4% a year, you need roughly 25× your annual expenses so the returns alone cover your life — without ever draining the principal. That "safe withdrawal rate" comes from the Trinity Study (1998), a landmark academic analysis of historical market data, and it's the foundation of the financial-independence (FIRE) movement.

Good to know

  • Bitcoin is highly volatile. Crypto can swing dramatically; treat its return as speculative.
  • Rates are defaults. Every return rate is pre-filled with a reasonable default and is fully editable — tune them to your own assumptions.
